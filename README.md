## What Is Muraena?

Muraena is an AI-powered sales intelligence tool built specifically for small sales teams, SDRs, and startups. The core database covers 180M+ B2B professionals, 30M companies, 280M emails, and 25M phone numbers. That's not a scrappy side project — it's a real database.

What makes it stand out from something like Apollo or ZoomInfo is the approach to search. Instead of clicking through dozens of filters to narrow down your prospect list, you define your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) in plain English, and Muraena's AI figures out the rest. You can also just drop in a competitor's domain and let it find lookalike companies.

Beyond raw contact data, the platform layers in:

- **Buyer intent signals** across 7,500 topics and 250 categories — so you can target companies actively shopping for solutions like yours
- **Technographic profiling** — see what tech stack a prospect is running
- **Funding insights** — filter for companies that just raised a Series A and actually have budget
- **Verified emails** built-in, so you're not paying for a separate verification tool

## What Real Users Are Saying

Reviews across G2, Capterra, and Software Advice paint a consistent picture. Here's the general vibe:

**The good:**
- AI search saves serious time — people describe going from hours of prospecting to minutes
- Data quality is competitive with premium tools, according to multiple reviews
- Buyer Intent and keyword search features are highlighted as genuinely unique at this price point
- Customer support is fast and responsive

One reviewer from Invity.io described it as "like ZoomInfo, but for startups." Alex Southworth, founder of EtymonAI and former VP at Dun & Bradstreet, put it plainly: imagine using RocketReach or ZoomInfo but paying under $500 for it.

**The honest cons:**
- No Chrome extension for direct LinkedIn prospecting (that's a real gap if your workflow is LinkedIn-heavy)
- Sign-in is Google-only — no email/password option
- Can't save searches natively in the platform, only export them
- As a younger product, it lacks some of the maturity and feature depth of Apollo or ZoomInfo

If you're an enterprise sales team that needs Salesforce enrichment, LinkedIn Sales Navigator integration, and dedicated account management — Muraena probably isn't your tool yet. But if you're a startup or small team trying to build an outbound pipeline without dropping four figures a month? It's genuinely worth a look.
